## Keyword Usage Guidelines

### For AppSource Listing
- Use the **3 primary keywords** in the AppSource keyword fields
- Incorporate Tier 1 and Tier 2 keywords naturally in the offer title and description
- Use Tier 3 keywords in supplemental product information

### For Marketing Materials
- Use Tier 1-3 keywords in white papers, brochures, and case studies
- Use Tier 4 keywords in industry-specific landing pages and materials
- Use Tier 5 keywords in technical documentation and governance guides

### For SEO (Web Properties)
- Target Tier 1-2 keywords for primary landing pages
- Use Tier 3-4 keywords for blog posts and content marketing
- Build long-tail content around Tier 5-6 keyword combinations
